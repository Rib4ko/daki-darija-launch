

# Daki AI — Landing Page Plan

## Design System
- Dark mode with deep navy/charcoal background
- Electric blue and purple accent gradients
- Glassmorphism cards (semi-transparent with blur backdrop)
- Clean modern typography (Inter or system sans-serif)
- Fade-in-on-scroll animations throughout

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- "Daki" logo on the left
- Minimal nav links: How It Works, Benefits
- "Join the Waitlist" CTA button on the right

### 2. Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Scale Your E-commerce in Morocco with Native Darija AI."
- Subheadline paragraph about COD confirmations and 24/7 support
- **Animated audio waveform** — glowing bars that pulse like an AI speaking
- **Typing effect subtitle** that loops: "Salam, m3ak Daki. Derti commande f'site lyoma, atwsl ghda!"
- Email input + "Join the Waitlist" button

### 3. How It Works (3-step flow)
- Horizontal cards with step numbers and icons
  1. **Connect** — Integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.
  2. **Automate** — AI calls customers in Darija to confirm orders
  3. **Scale** — Real-time dashboard for delivery statuses
- Glassmorphism card styling with connecting lines/arrows between steps

### 4. Benefits Section
- Three feature cards with minimalist Lucide icons:
  - **Slash Return Rates** — Catch fake orders before shipping
  - **Zero Human Bottlenecks** — Handle thousands of simultaneous calls
  - **Advanced NLP** — Built for Moroccan dialect nuances

### 5. Final CTA Section
- Repeated "Join the Waitlist" with email input
- Motivational tagline

### 6. Footer
- "© 2026 Daki AI" copyright
- Placeholder social links (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
- Minimal and clean layout
